Allfather’s Poetic Elixir (Spiced Apple Cider)

Odin is the god of poetry, and this warm, spiced drink evokes the inspiration of his poetic mead while being accessible as a non-alcoholic option.

Description:

A comforting, aromatic cider that warms the soul, perfect for storytelling by the fire.

Ingredients:

  • 1 gallon (3.8 liters) apple cider
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 6 cloves
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional, for sweetness)

Instructions:

  1. Pour cider into a large pot and add orange slices, cinnamon, cloves, star anise, and honey.
  2. Heat over medium-low, stirring occasionally, until steaming but not boiling (about 15 minutes).
  3. Strain out spices and serve warm in mugs.
